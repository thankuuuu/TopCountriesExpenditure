Top Countries Expenditures Visualization and Analysis

Overview

Welcome to top countries expenditures visualization and analysis website. This website contains the military, health, education expenditures along with GDP data of 15 countries including some G20 countries from 2010-2015. According to the unavailable data issue and the overabundant expenditures of big countries, this website is not include some top country such as US, China, UK data.

Purpose

The purpose of ths website is to visualize the data of top countries on their military, health, and education expenditures in more easily understood formatand and try to analyze the pattern and trend of these three kinds of spending.
